# HoloPulse Fit: Known Limitations

1. On device tuning pending. Counting and gesture thresholds are tuned from synthetic sequences and reasonable defaults. Real world lighting, camera angle, and body proportions may need threshold adjustments. All thresholds live in one constants file per engine (CountingConstants, GestureConstants, PoseConfidence) so tuning does not require logic changes.

2. Overlay to preview alignment is approximate. The pose overlay and Aura trails map normalized landmark coordinates directly onto the overlay size and mirror for the front camera. They are not corrected for the exact center crop of the FILL_CENTER preview, so alignment can drift slightly near the edges. This is acceptable for a demo overlay.

3. Push-up approximation is intentionally rough. A front or high camera angle makes push-up depth hard to measure, so results are labeled estimated when confidence is low, as required by the instruction document.

4. Session timer during background. The session timer and Ghost playback run on the ViewModel scope, so elapsed time can continue to accrue while the app is backgrounded even though the camera and pose processing pause. Counting and gestures do not advance without frames.

5. Side swipe semantics. Side swipe switches between exercises during a session. Because the front preview is mirrored, users may need a moment to learn the swipe direction. Touch previous and next controls are always available.

6. Ghost sample file. The provided ghost-trainer-sample.json contains a label and metadata but no coordinate frames. The demonstrable fallback geometry therefore comes from sample-motion-paths.json, which carries a real simplified squat series. Both files load through the same reader. See the Tracker decision log.

7. Screenshots and demo video. Screenshots of all screen areas and a demo recording of a full gesture controlled session are captured on a device and placed in 04_screenshots. They are not generated by the build.

8. Launcher icon. The launcher uses the generated adaptive icon. The provided brand logo is shown on the launch screen and in the app.

9. APK size. The debug APK is large (around 100 MB) because the ML Kit pose model and native libraries are bundled for on device inference with no network dependency.
